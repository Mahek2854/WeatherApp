Emergency Protocols

Quick reference guides for different weather emergencies to ensure the safety of your team and operations.

Severe Thunderstorms

  • Move all personnel indoors immediately
  • Avoid windows and metal objects
  • Unplug electrical equipment
  • Monitor weather alerts continuously

High Winds

  • Secure all outdoor equipment and signage
  • Close and lock all windows and doors
  • Avoid driving high-profile vehicles
  • Stay away from trees and power lines

Heavy Snow/Ice

  • Implement remote work policies
  • Clear walkways and parking areas
  • Check heating systems regularly
  • Maintain emergency supply kits

Extreme Heat

  • Increase air conditioning usage
  • Provide extra water for employees
  • Limit outdoor work activities
  • Monitor employees for heat exhaustion

Flooding

  • Move equipment to higher ground
  • Turn off electricity in affected areas
  • Evacuate lower levels if necessary
  • Document damage for insurance

Tornadoes

  • Move to lowest floor interior room
  • Stay away from windows and doors
  • Cover head and neck with hands
  • Wait for all-clear signal
